How to counter these hackers



There are various ways for hackers to get the personal information they need.

You must start implementing measures to fortify your online defenses and enjoy a safer and more secure online experience.





1. Keep your personal information secure


Use complex passwords and encrypting your data.

Use only secure wifi networks to send e-mails with personal information.

Don't leak your perosnal information over an e-mail or on social networking.

2. Two-factor authentication (2FA)


Implement two-factor aunthentication to avoid email spoofing.

Email spoofing is the fogery of an e-mail header to make it appear as if it is coming from a friend or employee.

Two-factor authentication provides protection from e-mail spoofing by reporting such attempts to users.

3. Security software


Download security software on your computer, such as antivirus, antispyware and firewall.

Security software is a protection that can help you defend your data, companies and systems.
